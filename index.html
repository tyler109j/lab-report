11 Promotion Criteria

Thresholds for each stage transition. A strategy cannot advance until every criterion in the current stage is met.

Research → Demo

  1. Backtest pass: n≥30, sharpe>0.5, WR>25%, DD≤30%
  2. OOS validates: forward_pass=1 on held-out window
  3. Code deployed to demo environment
  4. Deflated Sharpe documented (selection-bias corrected)

Demo → Micro-Live

  1. ≥100 paper trades
  2. ≥60 calendar days of paper trading
  3. Positive expectancy after costs (PF ≥ 1.20)
  4. Max DD within 30% threshold
  5. Kill switch tested (manual + auto)
  6. No missing SL/TP in paper_trades
  7. No critical errors for 14 consecutive days
  8. Deflated Sharpe documented

Micro-Live → Larger Allocation

  1. ≥50 live trades
  2. Real slippage within modeled range (±0.2 pip)
  3. Positive live expectancy
  4. No operational failures (API outages, crashes)
  5. No reconciliation mismatches
  6. Max observed DD < 20% on live
