🧪 Strategy families in rotation

Every day at 04:00 UTC, the system picks the next family from the queue, scaffolds a basic implementation (if not yet done), runs 30 random configs of it through the backtester, and rotates the queue. The 14-entry queue gives us ~2 weeks of new families to explore before any repeat. A family stays in the rotation as long as it's in the cycle, and the cycle weights families with no qualifying runs (n < 30 or Sharpe < 0.5) more heavily — underperformers get more attention.

⚠ What we don't know yet

The honest limits of this research. Read this before trusting any number above.

  • ▲Data window. 187 days of M15 candles. Need 1+ year to cover multiple regimes. Daily refresh cron is extending this.
  • ▲Live execution. 0 paper trades actually executed. 145 signals logged in dry-run only — no real OANDA fills, no real spread/slippage confirmation.
  • ▲Costs. Backtest applies modeled spread + swap (v2 costs). Not yet validated against live fills.
  • ▲Regimes. Window is mostly trending FX. No 2020-style vol spike or central-bank shock in the sample.
  • ▲Scope. 9 OANDA majors only. No crosses, exotics, or other asset classes.
