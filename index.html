What this is

Lab is the consolidated research project that replaced the three earlier lanes (quant_lab_fx, oanda-autoresearch, div_sweep) on 2026-06-13. The earlier projects hit the same wall: not enough trades on D/4h timeframes.

This project: 4 strategy families (divergence-at-S/R, mean reversion, breakout, confluence) on 9 OANDA majors at 15m primary + 1h trend filter. 15-min cron-driven research cycle.

Pass criteria: n≥30, Sharpe>0.5, WR>25%, max-DD≤30%.
